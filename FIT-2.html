Foundation in Business

This programme is the perfect jump start for school leavers keen to embark on a future career in IT. It prepares the student with top-of-the-line IT knowledge and skills to tackle a variety of industries and sectors. It gives the learner a theoretical foundation of IT, current systems, the role of information flow in various departments, systems development, implementation, evaluation and enhancement. The student will also learn about the impact of IT on human resources, companies and organisations. With a solid understanding of IT, the ambitious student may want to further his education and knowledge through a degree in IT later on. This sector is a minefield of opportunities for future web developers, software engineers, computer systems analysts, and many more promising careers.
